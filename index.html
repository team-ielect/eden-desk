How NOVA-EDEN Works

A streamlined process designed for efficiency and integrity. From setup to results in three simple stages.

Admin Setup

Admins create elections, configure settings, set timelines, and assign verified officers to oversee process.

Officer Verification

Designated officers verify candidate eligibility and voter rolls to ensure a legitimate pool of participants.

Unified Voting

Students login securely to a unified portal to cast their votes. Real-time encryption ensures anonymity.
